**AlgoYou**

“Don’t let your algorithm control you — take control of your own algorithm.”

**Description**

AlgoYou is a Chrome extension that gives users insight into their YouTube activity. It tracks what you watch and search, then generates a simple, bullet-pointed memo that highlights patterns and shows how algorithms may be shaping your recommendations. The goal is to put users back in charge of their digital experience.

**Features**

Tracks YouTube activity (watch/search history).

Generates easy-to-read summaries of your content consumption.

Highlights habits and viewing patterns.

Future vision: delete/edit stored data, add chatbot navigation, and provide feedback on harmful content.

How to Run Locally

Download or clone this repository.

git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/AlgoYou.git


Open the Chrome Extensions setup page
.

Go to chrome://extensions/ in your browser.

Turn on Developer mode (top right).

Click Load unpacked and select the downloaded folder.

AlgoYou should now appear in your Chrome extensions!
